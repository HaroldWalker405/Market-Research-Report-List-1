<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Polyester Staple Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent FRP Staple Fiber</li><li>Treated FRP Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polyester Staple Fiber market comprises two main types: Inherent FRP Staple Fiber and Treated FRP Staple Fiber. Inherent FRP Staple Fiber is manufactured from fibers that possess flame-retardant properties naturally, offering durable protection without additional treatments. In contrast, Treated FRP Staple Fiber is created by applying chemical flame-retardant treatments to standard polyester fibers, enhancing their fire resistance. Both types are used in various applications, including textiles and upholstery, where safety and compliance with fire regulations are essential.</p></p>
