<p><p>The preservation bag market comprises three primary types: PE (polyethylene), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), and PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride). PE bags are known for their flexibility and moisture barrier properties, making them suitable for general protective applications. PVC bags offer excellent clarity and strength, often used for packaging food and medical products. PVDC bags provide superior gas barrier characteristics, ideal for preserving freshness in food products. Each type caters to specific preservation needs, influenced by material properties and application requirements.</p></p>
