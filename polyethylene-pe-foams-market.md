<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) foams are categorized into two main types: Non-XLPE (Non-Cross-Linked Polyethylene) foam and XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) foam. Non-XLPE foam is known for its flexibility, lower density, and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for packaging and insulation applications. In contrast, XLPE foam features enhanced durability, moisture resistance, and thermal insulation properties due to its cross-linking process, making it ideal for automotive, construction, and medical applications. Each type serves distinct functional requirements across various industries.</p></p>
