<p><p>The OLED encapsulation materials market comprises several types, each serving unique protective roles. Rigid glass provides robust protection against moisture and environmental factors. Flexible glass offers durability while maintaining lightness and flexibility, ideal for portable devices. Conformal coatings apply thin layers that conform to surfaces, enhancing moisture resistance. Metal foils act as effective barriers against oxygen and moisture, while laminates combine layers of materials for enhanced protection and durability. Collectively, these options play a crucial role in prolonging OLED lifespan and performance.</p></p>
