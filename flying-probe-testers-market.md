<p><p>Flying probe testers (FPTs) are essential in the electronics industry for testing printed circuit boards (PCBs). The market is divided into two main types: single-sided and dual-sided probing testers. Single-sided probing testers focus on one side of the PCB, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity for less complex boards. In contrast, dual-sided probing testers simultaneously access both sides of the PCB, providing comprehensive testing capabilities. Dual-sided testers cater to more intricate designs, delivering higher accuracy and efficiency in quality assurance.</p></p>
