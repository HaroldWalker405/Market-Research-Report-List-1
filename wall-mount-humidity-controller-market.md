<p><p>The wall mount humidity controller market is primarily divided into two types: automatic and toggle controllers. Automatic humidity controllers use advanced sensors and algorithms to monitor and adjust humidity levels in real time, providing user convenience and precision. In contrast, toggle controllers offer a manual operation option, allowing users to set humidity levels through physical switches. While automatic controllers are favored for their ease of use and efficiency, toggle controllers appeal to those who prefer hands-on control over their humidity management.</p></p>
