<p><ul><li>Green</li><li>Half Speed Blue</li><li>Full Speed Blue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical X-ray films market is categorized into three main types: Green, Half Speed Blue, and Full Speed Blue films. Green films are sensitive to green light, typically requiring less radiation exposure, making them efficient for various imaging needs. Half Speed Blue films offer moderate sensitivity, providing a balance between image quality and radiation dose. Full Speed Blue films are highly sensitive to blue light, allowing for quicker imaging with reduced exposure but often at the cost of image clarity. Each type serves specific diagnostic requirements in medical imaging.</p></p>
