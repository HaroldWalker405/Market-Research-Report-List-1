Full dentures are designed to replace an entire arch of missing teeth, providing a complete solution for edentulous patients. In contrast, partial dentures are used when some natural teeth remain, offering support and functionality while preserving existing teeth. Both types benefit from 3D printing technologies, which enhance customization, fit, and comfort, while allowing for faster production and improved aesthetics compared to traditional methods.</p></p>
