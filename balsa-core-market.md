<p><p>Balsa core materials are categorized into two main market types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer balsa core consists of a single layer of balsa wood, offering lightweight and economical solutions for applications requiring structural integrity with minimal weight. In contrast, multilayer balsa core involves multiple layers, enhancing stiffness and strength while still maintaining a low weight profile. This multilayer design is ideal for high-performance applications, providing improved durability and resistance to delamination, making it suitable for aerospace and marine industries.</p></p>
