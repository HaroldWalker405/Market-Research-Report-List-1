<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Immunoassay</li><li>Boronate Affinity Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bench-top HbA1c analyzers market includes three main technologies: Ion Exchange Chromatography, Immunoassay, and Boronate Affinity Technology. Ion Exchange Chromatography separates different hemoglobin types based on their charges, providing accurate HbA1c measurements. Immunoassay employs antibody-based techniques to quantify HbA1c levels, offering rapid results. Boronate Affinity Technology relies on the unique binding properties of boronate molecules to selectively measure glycosylated hemoglobin, ensuring high specificity and sensitivity. These technologies contribute to effective diabetes monitoring and management.</p></p>
