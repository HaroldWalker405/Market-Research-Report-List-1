<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Firestop sprayable mastic is available in two main market types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component mastic is pre-mixed and ready to use, offering convenience and ease of application. It typically cures through air drying. In contrast, two-component mastic requires mixing of a resin and hardener before application, allowing for more customizable properties and often faster curing. Both types are utilized for fire protection in construction, ensuring effective sealing of joints and penetrations against fire and smoke.</p></p>
