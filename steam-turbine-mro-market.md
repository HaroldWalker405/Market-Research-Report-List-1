<p><strong>The Steam Turbine MRO Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Steam Turbine MRO (Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul) market encompasses various turbine designs based on flow direction and configuration. Axial turbines have steam flowing parallel to the shaft, optimizing efficiency in larger setups. Radial turbines direct flow perpendicularly, suitable for compact applications. Mixed turbines combine axial and radial flows for versatility. Tangential or helical turbines utilize curved paths to enhance performance in specific settings. Reentry turbines reintroduce steam for improved energy recovery, catering to specialized operational needs.</p></p>
