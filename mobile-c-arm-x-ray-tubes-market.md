<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile C-arm X-ray tubes market is primarily divided into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode systems. Stationary anode tubes utilize a fixed anode that produces X-rays with high stability, making them suitable for less demanding imaging applications. In contrast, rotating anode tubes feature an anode that spins during operation, allowing for improved heat dissipation and higher X-ray output, thus supporting more intensive imaging procedures. Both types cater to varying clinical needs in surgical and diagnostic environments.</p></p>
