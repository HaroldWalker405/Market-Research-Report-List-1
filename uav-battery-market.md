Li-Po batteries are known for their lightweight and flexible designs, offering high discharge rates ideal for racing and aerial photography drones. In contrast, Li-ion batteries provide higher energy density and longer cycle life, making them suitable for commercial and industrial UAV applications. Both types are essential for enhancing flight duration and overall performance, catering to various UAV operational needs in different sectors.</p></p>
