<p><p>The air-to-air heat exchangers market consists of two primary types: plate type and tube type heat exchangers. Plate type heat exchangers utilize thin, corrugated plates to create a large surface area for efficient heat transfer between air streams, making them compact and energy-efficient. Tube type heat exchangers, on the other hand, employ cylindrical tubes to facilitate heat exchange, offering robust construction suitable for higher pressure applications. Both types are essential for improving energy efficiency in HVAC systems and industrial processes.</p></p>
