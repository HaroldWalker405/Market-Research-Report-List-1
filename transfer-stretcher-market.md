<p><p>The transfer stretcher market is categorized into three main types: electric stretchers, hydraulic stretchers, and manual transfer stretchers. Electric stretchers offer powered lifting and adjustments for ease of use and patient comfort, making them ideal for frequent transfers. Hydraulic stretchers utilize fluid power mechanisms for height adjustment, providing a balance between efficiency and manual handling. Manual transfer stretchers rely on physical strength for adjustments, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but requiring more effort from caregivers. Each type serves distinct operational needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
