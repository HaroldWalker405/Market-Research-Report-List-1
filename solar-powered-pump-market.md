<p><strong>The Solar-powered Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Suction</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar-powered pump market comprises two main types: surface suction pumps and submersible pumps. Surface suction pumps are designed for drawing water from wells or ponds, utilizing solar energy to power the motor above ground. In contrast, submersible pumps are installed below the water surface, converting solar energy to operate while submerged. Both types are increasingly popular for irrigation and water supply in remote areas, offering efficient, eco-friendly solutions that reduce reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
