<p><p>The automobile exhaust catalyst market encompasses several types designed to reduce harmful emissions. The Three-way Catalyst (TWC) simultaneously converts carbon monoxide, hydrocarbons, and nitrogen oxides. The Four-Way Catalyst (4WC) adds particulate matter reduction. Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) targets diesel engine emissions, while Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) uses urea to convert nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water. Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) captures soot, and Ammonia Slip Catalyst (ASC) addresses excess ammonia. Other catalysts serve specialized applications in emission control.</p></p>
