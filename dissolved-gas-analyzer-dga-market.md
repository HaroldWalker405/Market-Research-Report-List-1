<p><ul><li>Vacuum Extraction or Rack Method</li><li>Head Space Extraction Methed</li><li>Stripper Column Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolved Gas Analyzers (DGA) are essential for monitoring the health of insulating oils in transformers and electrical equipment. The market types include Vacuum Extraction or Rack Method, which efficiently extracts gases by applying vacuum; Head Space Extraction Method, which analyzes gases present in the vapor space above the liquid; and Stripper Column Method, where gases are stripped from the oil using an inert gas. Each method caters to specific analytical needs, ensuring accurate assessment of transformer insulation conditions.</p></p>
