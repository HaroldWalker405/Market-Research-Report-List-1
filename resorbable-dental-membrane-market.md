<p><p>The resorbable dental membrane market consists of various types, primarily cowhide and pigskin membranes. Pigskin membranes, sourced from porcine origins, offer a flexible and biocompatible alternative, often favored for their natural collagen content. Both types are used in guided tissue regeneration, supporting healing while gradually resorbing in the body, thus minimizing the need for additional surgical interventions.</p></p>
