<p><ul><li>Fully Oxidized (Ubiquinone)</li><li>Semiquinone (Ubisemiquinone)</li><li>Fully Reduced (Ubiquinone)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10) is available in three market types based on its oxidation states. Fully oxidized CoQ10, known as ubiquinone, is the most common form, serving as a key antioxidant and energy producer in cells. Semiquinone (ubisemiquinone) represents a partially oxidized state, playing a role in electron transport. Fully reduced CoQ10, also known as ubiquinol, is highly bioavailable and effective in combating oxidative stress. Each form serves distinct functions in cellular metabolism and health supplements.</p></p>
