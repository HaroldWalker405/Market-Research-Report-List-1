<p><p>The Mercury Telluride Detectors market is primarily categorized into two types: photovoltaic and photoconductive detectors. Photovoltaic detectors generate an electrical signal when exposed to infrared radiation, making them highly effective for sensing applications. In contrast, photoconductive detectors change their electrical conductivity when illuminated, offering broad spectral response and fast response times. Both types find applications in fields such as infrared imaging, spectroscopy, and remote sensing, catering to various requirements in industrial, military, and scientific sectors.</p></p>
