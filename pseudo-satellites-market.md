<p><p>Pseudo satellites are innovative technologies that bridge the gap between traditional satellites and unmanned aerial systems. High-altitude pseudo satellites (HAPS) operate in the stratosphere, providing persistent surveillance, communication, and data collection over large areas without the latency associated with geostationary satellites. Ground pseudo satellites, on the other hand, leverage ground-based systems or tethered aircraft to deliver localized services and connectivity. Together, these market types offer unique capabilities for applications in telecommunications, disaster management, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
