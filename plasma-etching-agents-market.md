<p><p>The plasma etching agents market is categorized into two main types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents utilize liquid solvents to remove material from substrates through chemical reactions, providing high selectivity and isotropic etching. In contrast, dry etching agents employ gaseous or plasma states to achieve material removal, offering better control and directionality, essential for advanced microfabrication processes. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing and materials processing, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
