<p><strong>The Fetal Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Fetal Monitoring Devices</li><li>Internal Fetal Monitoring Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fetal monitoring devices are essential for assessing the well-being of the fetus during pregnancy and labor. They are categorized into two main types: external and internal fetal monitoring devices. External devices, such as Doppler ultrasound and electronic fetal monitors, assess the fetus's heart rate and uterine contractions through the mother's abdomen. In contrast, internal devices, like fetal scalp electrodes and intrauterine pressure catheters, provide more accurate measurements by being placed directly onto the fetus or within the uterus, allowing for continuous monitoring.</p></p>
