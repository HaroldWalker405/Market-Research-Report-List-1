<p><strong>The Medical Nitrogen Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSA</li><li>CMS</li><li>Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical nitrogen generator market includes three main types: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Cryogenic Air Separation (CMS), and Membrane Technologies. PSA uses cyclic pressure changes to separate nitrogen from air, making it efficient for on-site production. Cryogenic methods involve cooling air until separation occurs; it's suitable for large-scale needs. Membrane technology uses selective permeability to separate gases, offering a compact solution for smaller applications. Each type serves different healthcare settings based on scale, efficiency, and cost requirements.</p></p>
