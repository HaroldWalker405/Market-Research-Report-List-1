<p><p>The Tricuspid Valve Interventional Therapy market includes various techniques aimed at improving valve function and reducing regurgitation. Leaflet clamping involves mechanically tightening the valve leaflets to enhance closure. Annulus retraction focuses on narrowing the valve annulus, improving coaptation and overall valve effectiveness. Increasing the matching area aims to enhance the contact surface between leaflets, optimizing blood flow and reducing the risk of leakage. Together, these approaches contribute to advancing treatment options for tricuspid valve pathology.</p></p>
