<p><p>The forward-looking Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) camera market is divided into two main types: monocular and binocular cameras. Monocular cameras use a single lens to capture images, providing basic object detection and lane-keeping assistance. In contrast, binocular cameras utilize two lenses to achieve stereoscopic vision, allowing for depth perception and more advanced functionalities, such as 3D object recognition and enhanced spatial awareness. This differentiation enhances vehicle safety by improving the precision of driver-assist features.</p></p>
