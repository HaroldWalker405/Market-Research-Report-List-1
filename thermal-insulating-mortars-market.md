<p><p>The thermal insulating mortars market is categorized into two main types: inorganic insulating mortars and organic insulating mortars. Inorganic insulating mortars, primarily composed of materials like cement and minerals, offer high durability and fire resistance, making them suitable for demanding environments. Organic insulating mortars, on the other hand, utilize polymer-based materials that enhance flexibility and adhesion, ideal for residential applications. Both types serve to improve energy efficiency and thermal regulation in buildings, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
