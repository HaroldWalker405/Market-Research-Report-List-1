<p><strong>The Implantable Ventricular Assist Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LVADs</li><li>RVADs</li><li>BIVADs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable ventricular assist devices (VADs) market consists of three primary types: Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVADs), Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVADs), and Biventricular Assist Devices (BIVADs). LVADs support the left ventricle, enhancing blood flow for patients with heart failure. RVADs assist the right ventricle, vital for those with right-sided heart issues. BIVADs combine both functionalities, aiding patients with complete heart failure. Each device type addresses specific cardiac conditions, enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
