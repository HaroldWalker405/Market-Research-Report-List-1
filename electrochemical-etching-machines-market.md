<p><p>Electrochemical etching machines are categorized into three main types based on automation levels: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require operator intervention for the entire etching process, offering flexibility but demanding more labor. Semi-automatic machines streamline certain tasks, reducing manual effort while still requiring user input. Automatic machines take efficiency further, performing etching autonomously with minimal human supervision, making them ideal for high-volume production and consistent quality. Each type serves different operational needs and scale of production.</p></p>
