Other market options may include conventional synthetic disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs (DMARDs) and non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), offering additional treatment alternatives for managing symptoms and slowing disease progression in psoriatic arthritis patients.</p></p>
