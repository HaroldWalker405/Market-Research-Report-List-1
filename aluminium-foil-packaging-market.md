<p><p>The aluminium foil packaging market is categorized into three main types: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible packaging. Rigid aluminium packaging includes containers and trays that provide solid protection and structural integrity. Semi-rigid packaging features materials that can maintain shape yet allow for some flexibility, often used in food industry applications. Flexible packaging comprises thinner, pliable layers that adapt to various shapes, ensuring versatility and lightweight characteristics. Each type offers unique benefits for preserving product freshness and enhancing shelf appeal.</p></p>
