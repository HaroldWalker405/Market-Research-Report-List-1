<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Two Components</li><li>Three Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solvent-free resins market is segmented into single-component, two-component, and three-component systems. Single-component resins are ready to use and typically cure through exposure to heat or moisture. Two-component systems require mixing a resin and a hardener before application, allowing for enhanced performance and customization. Three-component systems add a third component, often a catalyst or modifier, to further enhance properties like adhesion and curing time. These types cater to various applications, including coatings, adhesives, and composites.</p></p>
