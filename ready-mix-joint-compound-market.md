<p><p>The Ready Mix Joint Compound market primarily consists of two types: Light Weight Ready Mix Joint Compound and Conventional Ready Mix Joint Compound. Light Weight variants are designed for reduced weight, making them easier to handle and apply, while also minimizing shrinkage and cracking. In contrast, Conventional Ready Mix Joint Compounds are typically heavier and offer good adhesion and durability but may require more effort during application. Both types are essential for drywall finishing in construction and repair projects, catering to different user preferences and requirements.</p></p>
